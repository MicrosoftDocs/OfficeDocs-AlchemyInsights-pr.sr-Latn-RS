# <a name="irm-protection-to-sharepoint-files"></a>IRM zaštita SharePoint datotekama


<p>U okviru SharePoint Online, IRM zaštita primenjuje se na datoteke na nivou liste i biblioteke. Vaša organizacija mogli da koristite uslugu IRM zaštitu, prvo morate podesiti Rights Management. IRM se oslanja na Azure Rights Management uslugu iz Azure zaštite informacija za šifrovanje i dodelite ograničenja upotrebe. Neki Office 365 planovi uključuju Azure Rights Management, ali ne sve. Biste saznali više, pročitajte <a href="https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/information-protection/understand-explore/office-apps-services-support" data-linktype="external">kako Office aplikacije i usluge podrške Azure Rights Management</a>.</p> <ul> <li><a href="https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/office365/securitycompliance/set-up-irm-in-sp-admin-center">Postavi se Information Rights Management (IRM) u programu SharePoint admin center</a></li> <li><a href="https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/office365/securitycompliance/set-up-irm-in-sp-admin-center#irm-enable-sharepoint-document-libraries-and-lists">IRM omogući SharePoint biblioteke dokumenata i liste</a></li> <li><a href="https://support.office.com/en-US/Article/Information-Rights-Management-in-Office-c7a70797-6b1e-493f-acf7-92a39b85e30c">Information Rights Management u sistemu Office</a></li> <li><a href="https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/office365/SecurityCompliance/information-rights-management-in-exchange-online">Information Rights Management u Exchange Online</a></li> </ul>
