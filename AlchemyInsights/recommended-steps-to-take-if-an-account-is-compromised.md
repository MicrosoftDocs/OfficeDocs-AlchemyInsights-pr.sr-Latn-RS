1. <span data-ttu-id="5a3f6-104">[Poništite lozinku korisnika](https://docs.microsoft.com/office365/admin/add-users/reset-passwords) odmah.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5a3f6-104">[Reset the user's password](https://docs.microsoft.com/office365/admin/add-users/reset-passwords) immediately.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5a3f6-105">Ne šaljite krajnjem korisniku novu lozinku putem e-pošte.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5a3f6-105">Do not communicate the new password through email to the end user.</span></span>
