# <a name="teams-meeting-recordings-to-onedrive"></a>Snimci sastanaka tima u OneDrive

Microsoft timovi imaju novi metod za čuvanje snimaka sastanka. Kao prvu fazu prelaza od klasičnog Microsoft toka do [novog toka](https://docs.microsoft.com/stream/streamnew/new-stream), ovaj metod skladišti snimke u usluzi Microsoft OneDrive for Business i sistemu SharePoint u usluzi Microsoft 365 i nudi mnoge beneficije.  

- [Podešavanje opcije snimanja sastanka za OneDrive for Business i SharePoint](https://docs.microsoft.com/MicrosoftTeams/tmr-meeting-recording-change#set-up-the-meeting-recording-option-for-onedrive-for-business-and-sharepoint)
- [Odbijanje usluge OneDrive for Business i SharePoint da biste nastavili da koristite protok](https://docs.microsoft.com/MicrosoftTeams/tmr-meeting-recording-change#opt-out-of-onedrive-for-business-and-sharepoint-to-continue-using-stream)  
- [Najčešća pitanja](https://docs.microsoft.com/MicrosoftTeams/tmr-meeting-recording-change#frequently-asked-questions)
