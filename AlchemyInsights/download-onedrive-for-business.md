# <a name="download-onedrive-for-business"></a>Preuzimanje usluge OneDrive for Business

Ako imate Windows 10, OneDrive je već instalirana na RAČUNARU. Ako koristite drugu verziju operativnog sistema Windows, instalirajte OneDrive da biste započeli.

- OneDrive for Business se može preuzeti  [ovde](https://www.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/onedrive/download).
- Kada se preuzmu i instaliraju, korisnici će se tražiti da se prijave na poslovni ili školski nalog.
- [Isključite, onemogućite ili deinstalirate OneDrive](https://support.microsoft.com/office/turn-off-disable-or-uninstall-onedrive-f32a17ce-3336-40fe-9c38-6efb09f944b0).
- Sinhronizovanje datoteka pomoću usluge OneDrive u [operativnom sistemu Windows](https://support.microsoft.com/office/615391c4-2bd3-4aae-a42a-858262e42a49) ili u [Mac računaru](https://support.microsoft.com/office/d11b9f29-00bb-4172-be39-997da46f913f).
