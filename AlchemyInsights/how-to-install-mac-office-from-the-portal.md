# <a name="how-to-install-mac-office-from-the-portal"></a>Kako da instalirate Mac Office sa portala

1. [Deinstalirajte Office 2011 za Mac sa računara](https://support.office.com/article/4bfcd230-0ea1-4656-bf30-dbfa44d358fa?wt.mc_id=Alchemy_ClientDIA).

2. [Upravljanje postavkama preuzimanja softvera](https://docs.microsoft.com/DeployOffice/manage-software-download-settings-office-365).

3. [Dodeljivanje licenci korisnicima](https://docs.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/admin/manage/assign-licenses-to-users).

4. Korisnici mogu da instaliraju i sami Office softver ili možete da koristite stavku [Primena sistema Office na Mac korisnike](https://docs.microsoft.com/DeployOffice/mac/deployment-guide-for-office-for-mac).
